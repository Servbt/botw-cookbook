Pantry Translation Guide

  • Tabantha Wheat: all-purpose flour, bread flour, pie crust, pasta, or cooked wheat berries
  • Hylian Rice: short-grain rice, sushi rice, arborio, jasmine, or calrose
  • Goat Butter: butter; goat butter if available
  • Fresh Milk: whole milk; oat milk for dairy-free versions
  • Rock Salt: kosher salt or flaky sea salt
  • Goron Spice: Japanese curry powder, garam masala, smoked paprika, chili, or house curry blend
  • Monster Extract: a dramatic sauce element: berry reduction, black garlic molasses, squid ink, balsamic glaze, or dark spice syrup
  • Hearty ingredients: nutrient-dense additions: avocado, egg, salmon, beans, mushrooms, or root vegetables
  • Critters / Monster Parts: for elixirs, translate to herbs, teas, spices, fruit, caffeine-free tonics, or sparkling drinks
