Chapter IV — Final Cooking Pot Meal Drafts

This batch completes the cooking-pot meal checklist: pepper dishes, prime meats, salted grills, seafood, steamed foods, vegetables, bread, milk, and crepes.

Pepper Seafood

Inspired by: Spicy Pepper; Any Seafood
Real-world version: spicy pepper seafood sauté
Makes/serves: 4 | Time: 20 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb shrimp, fish, scallops, or mixed seafood
  • 2 spicy peppers or 1 jalapeño, sliced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper

Method 1. Season seafood with salt and pepper. 2. Sauté peppers in oil for 2 minutes. 3. Add garlic and seafood; cook until just done. 4. Finish with lime juice and serve hot.

Pepper Steak

Inspired by: Spicy Pepper; Any Meat
Real-world version: pepper steak skillet
Makes/serves: 4 | Time: 25 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb steak strips or chicken thighs
  • 2 spicy peppers or bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Salt and black pepper

Method 1. Season meat with salt and black pepper. 2. Sear in oil until browned. 3. Add peppers, soy sauce, and honey. 4. Cook until glossy and the peppers soften.

Plain Crepe

Inspired by: Fresh Milk; Cane Sugar; Tabantha Wheat; Bird Egg; Goat Butter
Real-world version: basic butter crepes
Makes/serves: 8 crepes | Time: 30 min | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Method 1. Blend all ingredients until smooth. 2. Rest batter for 10 minutes. 3. Cook thin layers in a buttered nonstick pan. 4. Stack and serve with fruit, honey, or jam.

Porgy Meunière

Inspired by: Any Porgy; Goat Butter; Tabantha Wheat
Real-world version: butter-flour pan fish
Makes/serves: 2 | Time: 20 min | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 porgy, sea bream, or trout fillets
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon wedges
  • Parsley

Method 1. Season fish and dust lightly with flour. 2. Cook in butter until golden on both sides. 3. Spoon foaming butter over the fish. 4. Finish with lemon and parsley.

Poultry Curry

Inspired by: Raw Bird Drumstick; Hylian Rice; Goron Spice
Real-world version: simple chicken curry rice
Makes/serves: 4 | Time: 40 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken drumstick meat or thighs
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups stock
  • Japanese curry roux or curry powder slurry
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Oil

Method 1. Brown chicken in oil. 2. Add onion and cook until soft. 3. Add stock and simmer until chicken is tender. 4. Stir in curry roux and serve over rice.

Poultry Pilaf

Inspired by: Raw Bird Drumstick; Hylian Rice; Bird Egg; Goat Butter
Real-world version: chicken and egg butter pilaf
Makes/serves: 4 | Time: 40 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups rice
  • 1 lb chicken, diced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 1/4 cups chicken stock
  • 2 eggs, boiled or fried
  • Salt and pepper

Method 1. Brown chicken in butter. 2. Add rinsed rice, stock, salt, and pepper. 3. Cover and simmer 15 minutes; rest 10 minutes. 4. Serve with sliced boiled eggs or fried eggs.

Prime Meat and Rice Bowl

Inspired by: Raw Prime Meat; Hylian Rice; Rock Salt
Real-world version: seared prime meat rice bowl
Makes/serves: 2 | Time: 25 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 12 oz sirloin, pork loin, or chicken thigh
  • 2 cups hot cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional scallion

Method 1. Season and sear meat in oil. 2. Rest, then slice. 3. Melt butter in the pan with a pinch of salt. 4. Serve meat over rice with pan butter and scallion.

Prime Meat and Seafood Fry

Inspired by: Any Prime Meat; Any Seafood
Real-world version: prime meat and seafood skillet
Makes/serves: 4 | Time: 25 min | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil

Pumpkin Pie

Inspired by: Fortified Pumpkin; Tabantha Wheat; Cane Sugar; Goat Butter
Real-world version: spiced pumpkin pie
Makes/serves: 8 slices | Time: 1 hr 15 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust
  • 1 3/4 cups pumpkin purée
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup evaporated milk or cream
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • Pinch salt

Method 1. Heat oven to 375°F / 190°C. 2. Whisk filling ingredients until smooth. 3. Pour into crust. 4. Bake 45–55 minutes until just set.

Rock-Hard Food

Inspired by: Wood or ore
Real-world version: edible “rock-hard” seed crackers
Makes/serves: 1 tray | Time: 45 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup mixed seeds
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/3 cup water
  • Optional cracked pepper

Method 1. Heat oven to 350°F / 175°C. 2. Mix ingredients into a stiff dough. 3. Roll very thin and score into shards. 4. Bake 25–30 minutes until hard and crisp.

Inspired by: Hearty Salmon; Hylian Rice; Goat Butter; Rock Salt
Real-world version: salmon butter risotto
Makes/serves: 4 | Time: 40 min | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 8 oz salmon
  • 4 cups warm stock
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 1/4 cup parmesan
  • Salt and pepper

Method 1. Poach or sear salmon and flake it. 2. Cook shallot and rice in butter. 3. Add warm stock gradually, stirring until creamy. 4. Fold in salmon and parmesan.
