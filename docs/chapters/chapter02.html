Apple Pie

Inspired by: Tabantha wheat, cane sugar, goat butter, apple
Real-world version: buttery apple table pie
Makes: 6 slices | Time: 1 hr 20 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients - 1 double pie crust, homemade or store-bought - 5 medium apples, peeled and sliced - 1/2 cup brown sugar - 2 tbsp white sugar - 2 tbsp flour - 1 tsp cinnamon - Pinch of nutmeg - Pinch of salt - 2 tbsp butter, diced - 1 egg, beaten, for brushing

Method 1. Heat oven to 400°F / 200°C. 2. Toss apples with sugars, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. 3. Line a pie dish with crust, fill with apples, dot with butter, and cover with the second crust. 4. Crimp, vent, brush with egg, and bake 45–55 minutes until deeply golden. 5. Rest 20 minutes before slicing so the filling sets.

Camp note: Best eaten warm after a rainy ride between stables.

Carrot Stew

Inspired by: carrot, milk, butter, wheat
Real-world version: creamy carrot-potato stew
Serves: 4 | Time: 45 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients - 1 lb carrots, sliced - 1 small onion, diced - 2 tbsp butter - 2 tbsp flour - 3 cups vegetable or chicken stock - 1 cup whole milk - 1 small potato, diced - 1 tsp thyme - Salt and pepper

Method 1. Melt butter in a pot. Cook onion 5 minutes. 2. Stir in flour for 1 minute, then slowly whisk in stock. 3. Add carrots, potato, thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer 25 minutes. 4. Stir in milk and simmer 5 more minutes. 5. Mash a few carrots against the pot to thicken; serve with bread.

Clam Chowder

Inspired by: blueshell snail, milk, wheat, butter
Real-world version: coastal clam chowder
Serves: 4 | Time: 40 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients - 2 cans chopped clams, juices reserved - 3 tbsp butter - 1 onion, diced - 2 celery stalks, diced - 2 tbsp flour - 2 cups diced potatoes - 1 cup milk - 1 cup cream or half-and-half - Salt, pepper, parsley

Method 1. Cook onion and celery in butter until soft. 2. Stir in flour, then add clam juice and enough water or stock to cover potatoes. 3. Simmer with potatoes until tender, about 15 minutes. 4. Add clams, milk, and cream. Warm gently without boiling. 5. Season heavily with pepper and finish with parsley.

Crab Risotto

Inspired by: rice, butter, rock salt, crab
Real-world version: lemon-butter crab risotto
Serves: 4 | Time: 35 min | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients - 1 cup arborio rice - 6 oz crab meat - 4 cups warm seafood or chicken stock - 2 tbsp butter, plus 1 tbsp to finish - 1 shallot, minced - 1/2 cup white wine or extra stock - 1/4 cup parmesan - Lemon zest, salt, pepper

Method 1. Sauté shallot in butter. Add rice and toast 2 minutes. 2. Add wine and stir until mostly absorbed. 3. Add warm stock a ladle at a time, stirring until rice is creamy and tender. 4. Fold in crab, parmesan, final butter, lemon zest, salt, and pepper. 5. Serve immediately; risotto waits for no hero.

Meat Skewer

Inspired by: simple cooked meat
Real-world version: campfire beef skewers
Serves: 4 | Time: 30 min plus marinade | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients - 1 1/2 lb beef sirloin or chicken thigh, cubed - 2 tbsp soy sauce - 1 tbsp oil - 1 tbsp honey - 1 garlic clove, grated - 1 tsp smoked paprika - Salt and pepper

Method 1. Marinate meat with all seasonings for at least 30 minutes. 2. Thread onto skewers. 3. Grill or broil 8–12 minutes, turning often, until browned and cooked through. 4. Rest 5 minutes before eating.

Mushroom Skewer

Inspired by: mushroom-only cooking pot meals
Real-world version: garlic-soy grilled mushrooms
Serves: 4 | Time: 25 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients - 1 lb mixed mushrooms, large pieces - 2 tbsp melted butter or oil - 1 tbsp soy sauce - 1 garlic clove, grated - 1 tsp lemon juice - Black pepper

Method 1. Toss mushrooms with butter, soy sauce, garlic, lemon, and pepper. 2. Thread onto skewers or spread on a hot sheet pan. 3. Grill or roast at 425°F / 220°C for 15–18 minutes. 4. Finish with more lemon.

Pumpkin Stew

Inspired by: fortified pumpkin, milk, butter, wheat
Real-world version: creamy pumpkin stew
Serves: 4 | Time: 50 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients - 4 cups pumpkin or butternut squash, cubed - 2 tbsp butter - 1 onion, diced - 2 tbsp flour - 3 cups stock - 1 cup milk - 1/2 tsp sage - Salt and pepper

Method 1. Cook onion in butter until soft. 2. Stir in flour, then stock. 3. Add pumpkin, sage, salt, and pepper. Simmer until tender. 4. Stir in milk and lightly mash for a thick, rustic texture.

Seafood Paella

Inspired by: rice, butter, salt, shellfish, porgy
Real-world version: weeknight seafood paella
Serves: 4–6 | Time: 50 min | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients - 1 1/2 cups short-grain rice - 1/2 lb white fish, chunks - 1/2 lb shrimp or mussels - 3 cups seafood or chicken stock - 2 tbsp olive oil - 1 onion, diced - 2 garlic cloves, minced - 1 tsp smoked paprika - Pinch saffron or turmeric - Salt, lemon wedges

Method 1. Sauté onion in oil, then add garlic, paprika, and saffron or turmeric. 2. Stir in rice, then stock and salt. Simmer uncovered 15 minutes. 3. Nestle seafood on top. Cook 8–10 more minutes until rice is tender and seafood is done. 4. Rest 5 minutes and serve with lemon.

Monster Cake

Inspired by: wheat, sugar, butter, monster extract
Real-world version: black cocoa spice cake with berry “monster extract” glaze
Makes: 8 slices | Time: 1 hr | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients - 1 cup flour - 1/2 cup black cocoa or dark cocoa - 3/4 cup sugar - 1 tsp baking powder - 1/2 tsp baking soda - 1/2 tsp cinnamon - 1/2 tsp salt - 1 egg - 1/2 cup milk - 1/4 cup melted butter - 1/2 cup hot coffee or hot water - 1/2 cup berry jam, warmed, for glaze

Method 1. Heat oven to 350°F / 175°C. Grease an 8-inch cake pan. 2. Whisk dry ingredients. Add egg, milk, butter, and hot coffee or water. 3. Bake 28–34 minutes. 4. Cool, then glaze with warmed berry jam so it drips dramatically.

Chilly Elixir

Inspired by: cooling critter + monster part
Real-world version: cucumber-mint lime cooler
Makes: 2 drinks | Time: 10 min | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients - 1 cucumber, chopped - 10 mint leaves - Juice of 2 limes - 1–2 tbsp honey or simple syrup - Sparkling water - Ice - Optional: pinch of salt

Method 1. Blend cucumber, mint, lime, honey, and salt. 2. Strain into two ice-filled glasses. 3. Top with sparkling water. 4. Drink before crossing any desert, real or emotional.
