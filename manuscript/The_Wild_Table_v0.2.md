### Apple Pie
**Inspired by:** Tabantha wheat, cane sugar, goat butter, apple  
**Real-world version:** buttery apple table pie  
**Makes:** 6 slices | **Time:** 1 hr 20 min | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Ingredients**
- 1 double pie crust, homemade or store-bought
- 5 medium apples, peeled and sliced
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 2 tbsp white sugar
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Pinch of nutmeg
- Pinch of salt
- 2 tbsp butter, diced
- 1 egg, beaten, for brushing

**Method**
1. Heat oven to 400°F / 200°C.
2. Toss apples with sugars, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
3. Line a pie dish with crust, fill with apples, dot with butter, and cover with the second crust.
4. Crimp, vent, brush with egg, and bake 45–55 minutes until deeply golden.
5. Rest 20 minutes before slicing so the filling sets.

**Camp note:** Best eaten warm after a rainy ride between stables.

### Carrot Stew
**Inspired by:** carrot, milk, butter, wheat  
**Real-world version:** creamy carrot-potato stew  
**Serves:** 4 | **Time:** 45 min | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb carrots, sliced
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 3 cups vegetable or chicken stock
- 1 cup whole milk
- 1 small potato, diced
- 1 tsp thyme
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. Melt butter in a pot. Cook onion 5 minutes.
2. Stir in flour for 1 minute, then slowly whisk in stock.
3. Add carrots, potato, thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer 25 minutes.
4. Stir in milk and simmer 5 more minutes.
5. Mash a few carrots against the pot to thicken; serve with bread.

### Clam Chowder
**Inspired by:** blueshell snail, milk, wheat, butter  
**Real-world version:** coastal clam chowder  
**Serves:** 4 | **Time:** 40 min | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Ingredients**
- 2 cans chopped clams, juices reserved
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 2 tbsp flour
- 2 cups diced potatoes
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup cream or half-and-half
- Salt, pepper, parsley

**Method**
1. Cook onion and celery in butter until soft.
2. Stir in flour, then add clam juice and enough water or stock to cover potatoes.
3. Simmer with potatoes until tender, about 15 minutes.
4. Add clams, milk, and cream. Warm gently without boiling.
5. Season heavily with pepper and finish with parsley.

### Crab Risotto
**Inspired by:** rice, butter, rock salt, crab  
**Real-world version:** lemon-butter crab risotto  
**Serves:** 4 | **Time:** 35 min | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Ingredients**
- 1 cup arborio rice
- 6 oz crab meat
- 4 cups warm seafood or chicken stock
- 2 tbsp butter, plus 1 tbsp to finish
- 1 shallot, minced
- 1/2 cup white wine or extra stock
- 1/4 cup parmesan
- Lemon zest, salt, pepper

**Method**
1. Sauté shallot in butter. Add rice and toast 2 minutes.
2. Add wine and stir until mostly absorbed.
3. Add warm stock a ladle at a time, stirring until rice is creamy and tender.
4. Fold in crab, parmesan, final butter, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
5. Serve immediately; risotto waits for no hero.

### Meat Skewer
**Inspired by:** simple cooked meat  
**Real-world version:** campfire beef skewers  
**Serves:** 4 | **Time:** 30 min plus marinade | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Ingredients**
- 1 1/2 lb beef sirloin or chicken thigh, cubed
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 garlic clove, grated
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. Marinate meat with all seasonings for at least 30 minutes.

3. Grill or broil 8–12 minutes, turning often, until browned and cooked through.
4. Rest 5 minutes before eating.

### Seafood Paella
**Inspired by:** rice, butter, salt, shellfish, porgy  
**Real-world version:** weeknight seafood paella  
**Serves:** 4–6 | **Time:** 50 min | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Ingredients**
- 1 1/2 cups short-grain rice
- 1/2 lb white fish, chunks
- 1/2 lb shrimp or mussels
- 3 cups seafood or chicken stock
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- Pinch saffron or turmeric
- Salt, lemon wedges

**Method**
1. Sauté onion in oil, then add garlic, paprika, and saffron or turmeric.
2. Stir in rice, then stock and salt. Simmer uncovered 15 minutes.
3. Nestle seafood on top. Cook 8–10 more minutes until rice is tender and seafood is done.
4. Rest 5 minutes and serve with lemon.

### Monster Cake
**Inspired by:** wheat, sugar, butter, monster extract  
**Real-world version:** black cocoa spice cake with berry “monster extract” glaze  
**Makes:** 8 slices | **Time:** 1 hr | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Ingredients**
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup black cocoa or dark cocoa
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/4 cup melted butter
- 1/2 cup hot coffee or hot water
- 1/2 cup berry jam, warmed, for glaze

**Method**
1. Heat oven to 350°F / 175°C. Grease an 8-inch cake pan.

### Carrot Cake
**Inspired by:** wheat, cane sugar, goat butter, carrot  
**Real-world version:** small spiced carrot snack cake  
**Makes:** 8 squares | **Time:** 55 min | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Ingredients**
- 1 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/3 cup melted butter or neutral oil
- 1 1/2 cups grated carrot
- Optional: 1/3 cup walnuts or raisins

**Method**
1. Heat oven to 350°F / 175°C and line an 8-inch pan.
2. Whisk dry ingredients in one bowl and eggs, sugar, and butter in another.
3. Fold wet into dry, then stir in carrot and optional nuts or raisins.
4. Bake 28–35 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
5. Cool before slicing. Add cream cheese frosting if you want the stable-inn version.

### Crab Omelet with Rice
**Inspired by:** rice, egg, salt, crab  
**Real-world version:** crab fried rice under a soft omelet  
**Serves:** 2 | **Time:** 25 min | **Difficulty:** Medium

**Ingredients**
- 2 cups cooked rice, chilled if possible
- 4 eggs
- 4 oz crab meat
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1 scallion, sliced
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. Stir-fry rice in butter until hot and lightly toasted.
2. Fold in crab, soy sauce, scallion, salt, and pepper.
3. Beat eggs with a pinch of salt.
4. Cook eggs in a nonstick pan into two soft omelets.
5. Slide each omelet over a mound of crab rice.

### Cream of Mushroom Soup

**Real-world version:** rustic creamy mushroom soup  
**Serves:** 4 | **Time:** 35 min | **Difficulty:** Easy

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb mushrooms, sliced

- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp flour
- 3 cups stock
- 1 cup milk or cream

**Inspired by:** egg, milk, sugar  
**Real-world version:** baked vanilla custard

**Ingredients**
- 2 cups milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla

- Optional: caramel sauce

**Method**

- Pinch of salt
